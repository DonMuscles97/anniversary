June 20-24, 2019

This span of days was Firefly. Ok so on the Thursday we woke up and everyone linked at chik fila as usual; then we went to the check in which was pretty crazy becasue we more or less just drove through. We had about 6 or 7 campsites and after we got everything setup there was a small storm so we went in the tent and slept for a bit. The next day we ended up seeing zedd at the end of the night which is one of the best experiences I had at a concert. Hmmm what else happened? Pretty much the rest of the festival we just walked around and went to different concerts and stuff, I also happened to get of Firefly's instagram page for doing a backflip which I thought was pretty rad.
